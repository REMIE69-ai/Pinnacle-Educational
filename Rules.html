Rules and Regulations

Discipline, safety, time management, and respect are central to our learning culture.

1) Dress Code

Students are expected to dress decently and respectfully while at the center.

No damaged, transparent, or inappropriate clothing is allowed on the premises.

2) Time Management

Classes begin at 8:00 AM. Learners should be in the premises no later than 8:30 AM.

Lesson attendance is mandatory. Missed lessons should be followed up with the relevant teacher.

3) Discipline

Fighting, bullying, violence, disrespect, and defiance are strictly prohibited.

Serious indiscipline may lead to suspension or exclusion based on administration review.

4) Electronics

Parents are encouraged to limit gadgets brought to school unless necessary.

The center is not liable for loss of personal devices. Controlled access support is available for calls and research.

Reminder: These rules help create a safe, focused, and productive learning environment for every learner.
